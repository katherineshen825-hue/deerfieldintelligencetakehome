## Prototype Tool

**Deerfield Intelligence: Cross-Pod Capacity and Commitment Dashboard**

Link to the dashboard: https://deerfield-dashboard.lovable.app/

This tool gives the Deerfield Intelligence team a single shared view of everything both pods are working on to ensure there is visibility on capacity and deadlines.

Today, cross-pod coordination happens through Slack messages and gut feel. This dashboard replaces that with four simple views:

**Pod Capacity** shows at a glance whether each pod is under, at, or over capacity for the current two weeks. Green means bandwidth exists. Red means do not make new commitments without a conversation first.

**Active Projects** shows every project across both pods in one place, who owns it, how many hours it is consuming, and whether it is on track or blocked. Blocked projects surface automatically to the top so nothing gets buried.

**Cross-Pod Dependencies** shows every situation where one pod is waiting on the other to deliver something. If a dependency is approaching its deadline and is not resolved, it flags automatically in red before it becomes a crisis.

**External Commitments** shows every promise made to a portfolio company or investment partner, who made it, which pod is responsible, and whether it is on track. 

All four views are fully interactive. Anyone on the team can add projects, update statuses, log dependencies, and track commitments in real time. The goal is simple: before anyone makes a new commitment, they check the dashboard first.
